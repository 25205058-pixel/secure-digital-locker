# Usage Instructions for Secure Digital Locker

## User Registration
1. Go to the registration page.
2. Fill in your details (name, email, password).
3. Check the agreement box to accept the terms and conditions.
4. Click on the 'Register' button.
5. You will receive a verification email, click the link to activate your account.

## Login Process
1. Navigate to the login page.
2. Enter your registered email and password.
3. Click the 'Login' button.
4. If credentials are correct, you will be directed to your dashboard.

## Creating Records
1. Go to the "Records" section in the dashboard.
2. Click on the 'Create New Record' button.
3. Fill in the required fields in the record creation form.
4. Click 'Save' to store the new record.

## Viewing Records
1. Navigate to the "Records" section.
2. You will see a list of existing records.
3. Click on a record from the list to view its details.

## Searching Records
1. Go to the "Records" section.
2. Use the search bar at the top to enter keywords related to the record you want to find.
3. Press 'Enter' or click the search icon to display results.

## Updating Records
1. In the "Records" section, select the record you wish to update.
2. Click on the 'Edit' button.
3. Make the necessary changes in the form.
4. Click 'Update' to save changes.

## Deleting Records
1. Navigate to the "Records" section.
2. Select the record you wish to delete.
3. Click on the 'Delete' button.
4. Confirm the deletion in the prompt that appears.

## Backup Procedures
1. Go to the settings section.
2. Click on 'Backup Data'.
3. Choose the backup frequency (daily, weekly, monthly).
4. Click 'Start Backup' to initiate the backup process.

## Restore Procedures
1. In the settings section, click on 'Restore Data'.
2. Select the backup file from the provided list.
3. Click 'Restore' and confirm the action.
4. The system will restore your data from the selected backup file.

## Admin Panel Access
1. Navigate to the admin panel login page.
2. Enter the admin credentials (admin username and password).
3. Click 'Login' to access the admin dashboard.
4. From the admin panel, you can manage users, view logs, and access system settings.
